## Deployment(make copy)
*( work of these are same to make replica)

1. replicaSet :- It create exact same copy of the pod

2. StatefulSet :- It will gitve the numbering to the pod

3. Deployment(rolling updates) :- It updates pod one by one

* rolling updates:  In this all pods will not get updated at the same time, some gets update and some are running and vice-verca


** replication controller :- It contole or manages replication of pods
